`Untukmu Karyamu` is a hackathon project that automates website generation and deployment for MSMEs.

## Highlights

- Won 2nd place in Tencent Kepler Plan S3 Competition 2025, including Best Code, Best Product Idea, and Popularity Award.
- Architected a generative web-builder with Next.js and Gemini API.
- Automated deployment to Tencent EdgeOne to reduce launch time and infrastructure overhead.

## Tech Stack

Next.js, Supabase, Puppeteer, EdgeOne Pages.
